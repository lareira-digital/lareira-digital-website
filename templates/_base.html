Things should be simple

We've been suffering a lot of overengineering in the tech space for the past few years. You could say "always", since it's an inherent problem of our trade. This website is a statement to rethink web development overall. CSS, HTML and other base technologies have gotten really good but instead we are bloating everything with tens of megabytes and thousands of dependencies just to shave a few minutes of development, or, in some situations increase even more the development time because you have to learn that library/framework. We are becoming tool users rather than creators of the tools.

Please, for the sanity of the future, think twice before adding things you don't need to your software. We've been gifted with a wondrous brain, let's use it wisely.
