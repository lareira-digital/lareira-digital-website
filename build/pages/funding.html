All the public funds Lareira Digital has used in its history.

Updated on: 2023-07-16

As part as our ongoing commitment to transparency where a company should be transparent, we keep track of all the money that comes into Lareira that is not from pure work or projects revenue.

  • Public funding: Income from government programs (local, state or EU) to help companies in any way. These will be detailed with procedence and amount.
  • Donations: Income generated from donations made to the company. For obvious security reasons we will not disclose the donor, instead it will show the type, citizen or enterprise. Please note: As a for profit company, we pay our taxes for those donations.
  • External investment: Income from external investors. We will only disclose amounts and destination.

Public funding

Lareira digital has not taken any public funds for any projects.
